Nutrition Plan

Breakfast Options

  • Option 1: 70g oats, 150g frozen berries/banana/apple, 40g Iso whey
  • Option 2: Protein pancakes (50g oats, 1 full egg, 2 egg whites, 50 ml milk, 40g protein, salt, 100g berries/banana)
  • Option 3: Omelette (1 full egg, 5 egg whites, 50g red paprika, 100g champignons, half onion, spices, salad, brown bread slice)

Main Meals (split into 2-3 meals)

  • Carbohydrates: 280g rice, 650g sweet potato, 900g white potato
  • Proteins: 500g each of Chicken Breast, low-fat minced meat, beef steak, pork steak, 800g Scampi
  • Vegetables: 100g broccoli per meal

Pre-Sleep Meal

  • Cottage Cheese/Cesam: 200g

General Nutrition Guidelines

Rest Days: Eat half the carbohydrate amount

Post-Workout: 1 banana and 40g protein

Weighing: Food should be weighed uncooked

Salad and Cooking Method: Customize as preferred

Avoid Sugar: Including sugary drinks

Water Intake: 3-4 liters a day

Updates: Provide bi-weekly updates on weight and waist measurement

Cheat Meals: None allowed initially

General Workout Guidelines

Water Intake: 3-4 liters a day

Preworkout: 30 min before (if preferred)

Post Workout: 1 scoop Aminos (EAA or BCAA), Citrulline, 10g creatine

After Workout: 40g ISO Whey with 200 ml water

Technique: Use controlled weights for correct execution

Warm-up: Before every workout

Rest Day: Flexible (e.g., rest on Friday if tired and train on Saturday)

Motivational Note

Respect the plan and be patient. The results will take time, but with commitment and adherence to the program, success is achievable.
